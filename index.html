About Me

My name is Katie Patterson, and I am a Front-End Web Developer with an eye for design.

I am an enthusiastic Web Developer with a passion for client success. With over 10 years of experience in the design world and customer service/project management, I know the ins and outs when it comes to bringing an idea to fruition. My greatest joy in life is to see the smiles on my clients faces as they get to see their dreams become reality.

With a background in interior design ranging from retail, higher education, hospitality, and residential design, I will bring a unique perspective to any project. With a focus on user experience, I've been able to create designs that allow the users to instantly connect with the completed design. I focus on asking detailed questions to best understand how I can build a solution to the users challenges. This I believe creates a functional product that will last for years to come.
